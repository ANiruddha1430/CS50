Hollywood Guessing Game

Guess the movie name within 9 chances and show your movie knowledge

Rules of the Game

  • All the blanks together constitute a hollywood movie name.
  • You have 9 chances in total.
  • Each wrong guess costs you one chance.
  • When the lives run out the game ends and redirects you to the start page.
  • If there is a repeatation of letters filling once would fill all the positions the letter is at.
  • You can type one letter at a time or also guess the movie name by typing all the characters at once (of the given word length).
  • If all the characters entered are correct then you get maximum score and win the game.
  • If all the characters are entered at once and not correct then all the 9 lives are depleted and game is ended.
  • After finishing the game please click the Score button to view your score.
  • You can score a maximum of 100 points
